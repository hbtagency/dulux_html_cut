Mackay Junior Rugby League Community Project

A team of Perth Dulux Accredited Painters joined forces with Dulux Australia to repaint the interior of Cancer Council WA's Crawford Lodge.

It was extensive undertaking with Painters and Dulux staff giving their time over eight weekends, as part of their Community Giving Program.

The project represented over $140,000 in saved funds to the facility. This has allowed Cancer Council WA to invest these funds to support the one-in-two Western Australians that are living with cancer.
